FAQs

Is it safe to invest?

Keys3D is a game run on a Smart Contract, deployed on the Tron Network. Keys3D bears a mark possibility of one or more players being able to earn TRX, based on income to the game's Smart Contract.

All actions of each player are visible to everyone globally, via TronScan.org, and the Smart Contract source is verified, also - meaning anyone can audit the Smart Contract at any time. The Contract is immutable, so not even the creators can change the logic of the contract.



How do I interact with the game?

Since Keys3D is deployed on the TRON Network, you'll need a Dapp Wallet browser, or browser extension which is compatible with this chain. Such browsers, apps or extensions which allow full and proper use of the Keys3D game are freely available online - Arcadium Network recommends TronWallet for Android and iOS Users, and TronLink for Google Chrome and Firefox users.



How do I start making money?

In this game, you can make a profit in three ways: by buying Keys, attracting referrals and staking ATRC tokens to receive daily dividends. To increase the income from Keys, you can upgrade your Player Level.



How is income accrued?

Income is accrued every 3 seconds and is immediately available for withdrawal to your wallet.



How do I get Dividends?

Dividends will be paid automatically EVERY DAY - there is a timer available on the Dashboard which will indicate when payouts will occur.



Still have Questions?

If you have any questions, please feel free to join the Arcadium Network Discord server - there is an invite link on the Dashboard. One of the developers, or our participants will be happy to assist!



Please remember, this is a GAME - as such, there is NO GUARANTEE that you will profit from playing this game or activating its functions. Please only invest funds you can afford to lose - The developers and participants of Keys3D will not be held responsible for your loss of funds.
Upgrade Investor Level

To increase the income from Keys, you can upgrade your Investor Level. Each upgrade level adds 1 percent to the profit of each Key. When upgrading, all accrued profits are automatically transferred to the wallet at the current percentage, after which the percentage increases. In total, 5 upgraded Investor Levels are available.
